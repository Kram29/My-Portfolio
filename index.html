Name: Tanchuling College Student Monitoring System using RFID and with SMS Support
Category: VB.NET Application Development

I developed this system for our capstone project with the goal of enabling parents to monitor their child's academic performance and school attendance via SMS notifications. This system aims to provide real-time updates and ensure parents stay informed about their child's educational journey effortlessly. In this system, the faculty can log in to see their class schedule and add grades for their students. Students can also log in to see their class schedule and their grades.
